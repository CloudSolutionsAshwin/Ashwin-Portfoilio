Cloud Dreams to Reality: My AWS Cloud Practitioner Journey

By Ashwin Sharma

In this blog, I'll take you on a personal adventure through the world of AWS (Amazon Web Services), sharing the highs, lows, and invaluable insights I gained as I transformed my cloud computing dreams into a reality.

Cloud computing has revolutionized the way businesses operate and individuals access and store data. AWS, as one of the leading cloud service providers, has been at the forefront of this transformation. As I embarked on my journey to become an AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, I encountered challenges, celebrated successes, and learned more than I could have ever imagined.

This blog is not just about the destination; it's about the entire journey. My aim is to guide you through the process, provide resources, and share the strategies that helped me succeed in passing the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner exam. Whether you're just starting your AWS journey, in the middle of your preparations, or seeking inspiration to take that first step, this blog is for you.

Passing the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner exam is a significant step in advancing my career in AWS. It has given me more confidence, enabling me to progress further along this path.

Common Questions:

Question: Where should I start my preparation?

I began my preparation by enrolling in a Udemy course offered by Stephane Maarek. Additionally, for a more comprehensive understanding in Hindi, you can refer to the Technical Gufgtu YouTube channel.

Question: How about practice tests?

Once you've completed the course, it's crucial to evaluate your knowledge through practice tests. Begin with tests from Stephane Maarek and Abhishek Singh. Following that, consider the Tutorial Dojo practice tests; these are relatively easier and can significantly boost your confidence.

Question: How much time is needed for preparation?

The amount of time required for preparation varies from person to person, depending on your grasp of the material and the time you can dedicate to studying. In my case, I dedicated one month to thorough preparation.

Important Points: It's crucial to attend all the labs and gain hands-on experience. Practical experience is invaluable as exam questions often present scenarios that involve different services and situations. A solid understanding of the topics is essential to answer these questions accurately.

I'm sharing these insights based on my personal experience. Remember, there are multiple resources available for the exam, and you can choose the ones that align with your learning style. If you still have doubts, feel free to reach out to me directly.

Happy learning!
