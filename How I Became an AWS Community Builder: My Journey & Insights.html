My Journey to Becoming an AWS Community Builder
2021: The Beginning

My cloud journey started in 2021 when I took my first role as a Cloud Engineer Trainee. I focused on small daily tasks, hands-on implementation of AWS services, and continuous learning. Over time, I earned my AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner and later the AWS Solutions Architect certification.


2022: Community Engagement & Blogging

Being from Ahmedabad, Gujarat, I actively joined and volunteered at AWS Community Ahmedabad. Engaging with the community was a game-changer for me. I started writing blogs on AWS services, daily tasks, and troubleshooting experiences. I firmly believe that knowledge grows when shared, so I committed to continuous learning and giving back.
